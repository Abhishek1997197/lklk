Lion

Type: Mammal

Category: Carnivore

Foods: Meat, Buffalo, Deer

The lion is known as the king of the jungle. It lives in groups called prides and is found mainly in Africa and a few parts of India.

Tiger

Type: Mammal

Category: Carnivore

Foods: Deer, Wild Boar

The tiger is the national animal of India. It is known for its stripes and is an excellent swimmer. Tigers prefer forests with plenty of cover.

Elephant

Type: Mammal

Category: Herbivore

Foods: Grass, Fruits, Bark

Elephants are the largest land animals on Earth. They use their trunk to drink water, grab things, and even greet each other. They live in herds and are very social.
